About Me

I received my Ph.D. from the University of New South Wales under the supervision of Prof. Zhao Yang Dong and A/Prof. Guo Chen. Before joining HKUST(GZ), I served as a Postdoctoral Researcher at both the University of New South Wales and the University of Sydney, collaborating with Prof. David J. Hill and Dr. Fengji Luo. My research aims to enhance the integration of renewable energy sources into the grid and achieve low-carbon energy and transportation systems.

Research Interests

My research focuses on flexible resource management in smart grids, AI-driven autonomous energy systems, energy transactions, transportation electrification, and the application of optimization theory, game theory, and computational intelligence in these areas.

The key areas of my work include:

  • Smart Grid Flexible Resource Management (Electric Vehicles, Microgrids, Batteries, etc.)
  • AI-Driven Autonomous Energy Bidding and Management System
  • Energy Transaction
  • Transportation Electrification
